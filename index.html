What Is a Bra Size Calculator?

Using basic body measurements, usually your bust size (the largest area of your chest) and band size (underbust), a bra size calculator is an online application that helps you find your exact bra size. The calculator calculates your band and cup size, such as 34B or 36D, based on these figures.

how to measure bra size?

It is quick and simple to use a bra size calculator. Typically, you must accomplish the following:

1. Measure your band size

  • Stand straight and wrap a soft measuring tape around your ribcage, just under your bust.
  • Make sure the tape is snug but not tight.
  • Round to the nearest whole number.

2. Measure your bust size

  • Wrap the tape around the fullest part of your bust (usually at nipple level).
  • Keep the tape level and not too tight or too loose.
  • Again, round to the nearest whole number.

3. Enter your measurements

  • Input these numbers into the bra size calculator.
  • The calculator will compute your band and cup size using standard formulas.
